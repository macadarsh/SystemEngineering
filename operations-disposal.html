Operations, Maintenance & Disposal

The longest part of most systems’ lives: using, sustaining, and eventually retiring them.

Transition to operations

Handover moves the system from the development team to operators and maintainers, with the training, documentation, spares, and support arrangements needed to run it.

Sustainment and maintenance

In service, the system is maintained, upgraded, and monitored. For many systems this phase dominates total lifecycle cost, which is why it must be designed for from the start.

Retirement and disposal

Eventually the system is withdrawn. Responsible disposal addresses safety, environmental impact, data, and the migration of users to whatever replaces it.
