How to use this plan

Each week pairs a chapter (or part of one) with a short, concrete task. The goal is steady progress and a habit of applying ideas, not racing to the end. Use the final week to consolidate before moving on.

Tips for sticking with it

  • Protect a fixed slot each week — consistency beats intensity.
  • Always finish a week by doing, not just reading.
  • Keep a running list of questions to revisit in review weeks.
